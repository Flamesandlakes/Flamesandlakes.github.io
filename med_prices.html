What is the price of medicine?

As both the price of medicinal products and the products themselves change over time, I will start by looking at price based on the average price of the product across its time on the market.
This allows me to better compare products that may not otherwise appear on the market in the same time frames.

For some descriptive statistics, the range of average sales prices ranges from 0.05 DKK per DDD to as high as 2,370,000 DKK per DDD in some outlier cases.
The mean of the price range is 732.16 DKK per DDD whereas the median lies at just 12.88 DKK per DDD.

I will first add that the mean price of individual products does not necessarily reflect medicine costs for the average consumer as it does not factor in weights based on how widely used the given product is.

The sudden increase in mean price in October of 2020 can be attributed to the introduction of Idefirix to the market alongside a range of other highly priced products (albeit not as expensive as Idefirix).
